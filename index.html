Our thesis

We think like hotel operators, not utility companies. The stop should be worth the time.

Every Rangeway location is designed the way a hospitality operator designs a property. Lighting, materials, service, sequence. The charging happens inside a space built for the people waiting, not outside in a lot built for the car.

Our Story

Hospitality DNA meets EV passion.

Rangeway sits at the intersection of two worlds. One is a long career in hospitality, across luxury resorts, wellness retreats, and flagship urban hotels, where we learned what a great stop feels like. The other is a deep presence in the EV driver community, leading clubs and hosting podcasts, where we learned what charging could be. Rangeway is what happens when those worlds meet.
